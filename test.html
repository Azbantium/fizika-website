Physics Olympiad for Everyone

FIZIKA (est. May 2025) is a youth-led educational nonprofit run by a few pre-university students with a passion for problem-solving. We nurture students' interest in STEM at all levels, preparing them for the Singapore Physics Olympiad and partnering with schools for outreach.

In Collaboration With

FIZIKA is proudly endorsed and fully funded by the National Youth Council (NYC) Singapore’s Young ChangeMakers grant. This partnership allows us to keep all our programs free and accessible for students and expand our outreach across schools.
